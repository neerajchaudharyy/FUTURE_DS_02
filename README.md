# FUTURE_DS_02
# 📊 Netflix Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **customer churn patterns** for a subscription-based streaming platform inspired by Netflix. The goal of this analysis is to identify customer segments that are more likely to discontinue the service and to understand the **impact of churn on business revenue**.

The dashboard provides **interactive visualizations** to explore churn distribution across **age groups, genders, regions, and other customer attributes**. These insights help businesses make **data-driven decisions** to improve customer retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* 🔍 Identify customer churn trends and patterns
* 👥 Analyze churn distribution across **age groups, gender, and regions**
* 💰 Evaluate the impact of churn on **overall revenue**
* 📈 Provide insights that can help **reduce customer loss**

---

* 📱 **Device Type**

* 🚻 **Gender**

These filters allow users to **dynamically explore customer churn patterns** across different customer segments.

---

## 🔑 Key Insights

* 📉 The overall **churn rate is 50.30%**, indicating that a large portion of customers have discontinued the service.
* 👨 Adults represent the **highest churn segment (46%)**, making them the most critical group for retention strategies.
* 🌍 Certain regions such as **South America and Europe** show higher churn concentration.
* ⚖️ Churn distribution is relatively **balanced across male, female, and other gender groups**.
* 💸 A significant amount of **revenue loss (3.30M)** is associated with customer churn.
